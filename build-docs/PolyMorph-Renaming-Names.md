**PersiMorph** actually has something.

It hints at **persistent + morph**, which maps pretty well to what the system is: adaptive, ongoing, stateful. Not bad. Weird enough to be brandable, clear enough to remember. That’s rarer than startup people like to admit.

The only caution: **“Persi”** can sound slightly soft or ambiguous on first hearing. Some people will hear **PercyMorph**, **PursiMorph**, or think it’s a typo. Not fatal, just something to test out loud.
